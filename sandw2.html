Sandwiches and Lunch Options

The featured sandwich was my first PHX creation several years ago. I decided I was bored with the same old grilled cheese and decided to add turkey bacon (optional) to my sandwich and then decided to add seasoning to the butter on the bread. It is EVERYONE's favorite in my family and my circle of friends. I hope you enjoy it as well!


Jon's World Famous Grilled Cheese
Number Measurement Ingredient
1 2oz. Butter
2 1 Slice of Cheddar Cheese
3 1 tsp. Oregano
4 1 tsp. Garlic
5 1/4 cup Shredded Mixed Cheese
6 2 slices Any Bread of Your Choice
Step Directions
1 Mix butter, garlic, and oregano together in a small dish. Spread on one side of each piece of bread so that it covers the entire piece of bread.
2 Put one slice of bread, buttered side down, on to a skillet without heat on. Add sliced cheese and shredded cheese.
3 Turn on the stove to medium heat so that it does not cook too fast.
4 Flip once the first side is golden brown. Be careful since the shredded cheese may not be melted yet and could fall out if not careful.
5 Once the second side is golden brown and cheese is completely melted, serve warm to guests or yourself!
